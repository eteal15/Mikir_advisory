⚠️ Important Notice

How It Works እንዴት ይሰራል

Simple Process

ቀላል ሂደት — ምዝገባ ከ5 ደቂቃ ያነሰ ነው

1
Choose Your Service
አገልግሎትዎን ይምረጡ
Browse our 4 services (A, B, C, D) and select the plan that fits your situation and budget.
2
Fill the Application Form
ቅጹን ይሙሉ
Tell us your name, contact, situation type, and describe your problem in detail. The more you share, the better our advice.
3
We Review Your Application
ማመልከቻዎን እናጠናል
Your application is sent securely to us. We'll review it and confirm the correct service tier within a few hours.
4
Pay via Telebirr or CBE Birr
ቴሌብር ወይም CBE ብር ይክፈሉ
We'll send you the payment details. Transfer via Telebirr or CBE Birr and send us the screenshot. Payment required before work starts.
5
Receive Your Advisory
ምክርዎን ይቀበሉ
Your full written advisory is delivered via Telegram within 24 hours (or 48hrs for Deep Sessions). Private, clear, actionable.
6
Optional: Subscribe for More
ምርጫ፡ ወርሃዊ ምዝገባ
Loved the service? Subscribe to a monthly plan and always have an advisor available when life gets complicated.
